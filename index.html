Essential Travel Notes

A few tips to ensure your parents and you have the most comfortable experience.

Weather in May

Days are pleasantly warm (15°C - 22°C), but nights in Gulmarg get chilly. Layering is key. A light jacket for Srinagar and a heavy jacket/thermals for the Gondola phase 2 are essential.

Luggage & Mobility

Houseboat access requires stepping into a Shikara. Pack one overnight bag for the houseboat to avoid carrying heavy suitcases across the lake. Minimal walking involved.

Connectivity

Only postpaid mobile connections (Jio, Airtel, BSNL) work in Kashmir due to security regulations. Ensure at least one member has a postpaid SIM before traveling.
